Brand Social Media Campaign

#DegreeDost

"A jigri you always needed for seamless learning!"

A full-funnel social media campaign for Online Manipal designed to bridge the trust gap between online degrees and audience scepticism — particularly from families unconvinced of the value of online education. From ideation to execution, the campaign leveraged cultural resonance and authentic storytelling to shift brand perception at scale.

Consumer Insight

"Families and peers don't trust online degrees — and that distrust blocks enrolment decisions."

Strategic Objective

Target audiences actively considering degree courses (online or offline) and demonstrate that Online Manipal's programmes match the quality and career outcomes of traditional degrees — without compromising on flexibility.

Content Strategy

Three-phase content approach — building anticipation, launching impact, sustaining engagement

Phase 01 — Pre Launch

Building Buzz

  • Interactive Stories around the "jigri" concept
  • Trending-format Reels about jigris
  • Videos: Office jigris & Mothers as jigris
  • Learner testimonial teasers

Phase 02 — Launch

The Big Reveal

  • Animated hero video ad
  • Full campaign hashtag activation
  • Cross-platform push across Instagram & Facebook

Phase 03 — Post Launch

Sustaining Momentum

  • Programme promotional posts
  • Mentor & learner testimonials
  • Informative Reels & memes
  • Online Manipal Plus Series
  • Learner testimonial GIF series
